Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

GarTrack ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application GarTrack. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information unless you choose to contact us via email or support.
  • Device Information: We may collect device-related information (such as device type, OS version, and anonymous identifiers) for performance and analytics.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage data to improve user experience, including app interactions, feature usage, and crash reports.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the app
  • To improve and personalize the user experience
  • To monitor usage and analyze trends
  • To detect, prevent and fix technical issues
  • To offer support and respond to user inquiries

3. Third-Party Services

GarTrack may use third-party services that collect information used to identify you:

  • Google AdMob (for displaying ads)
  • Apple iCloud (for optional backups)
  • RevenueCat (for in-app purchases and subscriptions)
  • Crashlytics (for crash reporting)

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on your device and optionally in your iCloud account. We do not store your data on our servers. We use industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent to data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at support@gartrack.app.

6. Children's Privacy

GarTrack is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the app and/or on our website. Your continued use of the app after any modifications constitutes your acknowledgment and acceptance of the new policy.
